Nitterium is a modern, native Android app that provides a clean, privacy-respecting way to consume Twitter/X content. By leveraging Nitter instances, it avoids tracking and requires no user account, all wrapped in a beautiful Jetpack Compose interface.

This app allowing you to browse Twitter/X content without an account and subscribe to your favorite user profiles.

Important: This depends entirely on Nitter persistence and the operational state of public Nitter instances. It's important to check the source and health of the instance you are using.

Features:
* Privacy First: No ads, tracking, or account required.
* Nitter Integration: Browse via your preferred instance.
* Subscriptions: Save and re-arrange accounts locally via drag-and-drop.
* Image Viewer: Swipe to dismiss and direct downloads.
* Pull-to-Refresh: Swipe down to reload content seamlessly.
* Dynamic Theming: Material 3 design adapts to system theme.
* Modern UI: Fast and responsive native interface.
* Deep Linking: Intercepts Twitter/X/Nitter links automatically.
* RTL Support: Supports RTL content by default.
* FOSS: 100% open-source software.
